About Me

Brian Beaudoin (a.k.a. “The Stoner Dad”) is a lot of things. He’s a father, a storyteller and a hippie who lives with intense ADHD. He makes it all work by getting high and entertaining audiences with his hysterical and truthful stand-up comedy. Brian has twice been named “Best Comedian” in Rhode Island by the Providence Phoenix, can be heard weekly on the “Stoner Dad Podcast” and has been featured on SiriusXM, MTV and NESN.

Where Brian shines brightest though is in his live performances. Just like his favorite musical acts Phish, The Grateful Dead and Twiddle, every show is fantastic, but no two shows are the same.
